About HOMSA

Our Story

Home Muslims Association (HOMSA) was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a supportive community where Muslims can grow spiritually, access education, and serve humanity.

What began as small gatherings of families has grown into a vibrant association serving the Muslim community through various programs and initiatives.

Today, HOMSA stands as a beacon of Islamic values, community service, and youth empowerment in Uganda.

Our Mission

To foster a strong, educated, and spiritually conscious Muslim community through Islamic education, charitable works, and youth development programs.

Our Vision

A united Muslim community in Uganda that practices Islam holistically and contributes positively to society.
